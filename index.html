Platform Expertise: WordPress, Shopify, Squarespace & Wix

Glendale web design companies should be fluent in the platforms most relevant to today’s digital businesses. Here's how each platform fits into various business needs:

  • WordPress: Ideal for content-heavy websites, portfolios, blogs, and businesses looking for SEO scalability. Over 43% of all websites globally are powered by WordPress, thanks to its flexibility and plug-in ecosystem.
  • Shopify: If you're building a product-based online store, Shopify offers secure transactions, inventory management, and scalable sales channels. According to BuiltWith, over 4 million websites use Shopify, many of which are run by small and mid-sized businesses.
  • Squarespace: Great for personal brands, freelancers, and small service businesses. Squarespace is known for visually compelling templates and ease of use, especially for those who want to manage their site post-launch without hiring a developer.
  • Wix: Another beginner-friendly platform offering quick setup and drag-and-drop customization. Wix serves over 200 million users worldwide and is ideal for businesses that need simplicity and speed over complex functionality.

A credible Glendale agency should not only offer multiple platform choices but also explain why a specific one fits your needs—based on scalability, maintenance, and budget.

Design and search optimization are no longer separate tasks. Google’s Core Web Vitals and mobile-first indexing mean that speed, structure, and usability directly affect rankings.
